# Getting Started

modecat_ is a browser-based music tracker. With a free modecat account you can save your
projects to the cloud, access them from any device, and share them with others.

## Create an account

1. Go to [app.modecat.net](https://app.modecat.net) and click **Sign up**.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password — or sign in with GitHub or Google.
3. Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link inside.

That's it. Your account is ready.

## Connect the tracker

The tracker at [modecat.net](https://modecat.net) and your account at
[app.modecat.net](https://app.modecat.net) are separate apps. To link them:

1. Open the tracker at [modecat.net](https://modecat.net).
2. Click the **Connect account** button in the tracker toolbar.
3. You'll be redirected to the login page. Sign in (or your existing session will be used).
4. You'll be sent back to the tracker, now connected.

Once connected, you'll see a **Save to cloud** button in the tracker. Your session stays
active until you sign out.

## Save your first project

1. Build something in the tracker.
2. Click **Save to cloud** in the toolbar.
3. Give your project a name when prompted.
4. The project is uploaded and appears in your [Projects dashboard](https://app.modecat.net/dashboard/projects).

## Load a saved project

1. Open the tracker.
2. Click **Load from cloud** and pick a project from the list.
3. The tracker loads the project exactly as you left it.
